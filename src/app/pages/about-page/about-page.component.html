About Us...

Welcome to the Raleigh Beer Garden where we present to you the current world record holder for the world's largest selection of draft beer! We also offer a full menu of sandwiches, pizzas, salads and much more.

The Raleigh Beer Garden is pleased to offer a variety of seating options including our full service indoor and front porch seating as well as a "seat yourself/order at the bar" style outdoor setting in the back garden as well as the rooftop patio.


We also have Growlers available, so you can get your favorite draft beers to go! Come enjoy the show out back in our events area, toss a game of cornhole or play some ping pong with your friends.

We look forward to seeing everyone come down and experience the Raleigh Beer Garden in Glenwood South.

Cheers!
